Section 02

Privacy Policy

1. Data We Collect

Signal5 collects only the minimum information necessary to provide our specialist consultancy. This includes your legal name, professional email address (hello@signal5.io), and any AWS IAM Role credentials you provide during onboarding. All payment information is processed securely by Stripe; Signal5 does not store or have access to your full credit card details. We may log your connecting IP address solely for the purpose of technical support and troubleshooting your US Digital Bridge.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provision, manage, and secure your private network infrastructure. We use your contact details to communicate regarding your account status, billing, and mandatory technical updates. Signal5 operates a strict no-logging policy regarding your personal browsing traffic; we do not monitor, record, or inspect the content of the data moving through your AWS bridge.

3. Data Retention

Technical and account data is retained for the duration of your active service contract. Upon termination of your monthly retainer, your AWS IAM credentials will be purged from our systems within 30 days. We retain basic billing records for up to 6 years to comply with Irish Revenue and legal requirements.

We utilize industry-leading third-party processors to deliver our services, including Stripe for secure payment handling and Amazon Web Services (AWS) for infrastructure hosting. Signal5 does not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third-party marketing or data-mining firms.

You also have the right to data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with the Irish Data Protection Commission (DPC). Section 03

Refund Policy

Setup Fee

The €160 Entry Bundle (covering the technical setup and the first 30 days of service) is non-refundable once the 1-on-1 technical onboarding call has commenced and the AWS environment has been configured. Because this fee covers dedicated consultancy time and the manual provisioning of cloud infrastructure, all sales are final upon handover of the bridge credentials. If a client cancels their appointment at least 24 hours before the scheduled onboarding call, a full refund will be issued.

Monthly Retainer

The €49 Monthly Management Retainer is billed in advance every 30 days. These charges are non-refundable once the billing cycle has started. To avoid future charges, clients must provide notice of cancellation at least 7 days prior to their next scheduled billing date. Upon cancellation, your private AWS bridge and IAM access will be decommissioned at the end of the current billing period.

How to Request a Refund

To request a pre-onboarding refund or to cancel your ongoing retainer, please email support@signal5.io with your name and order reference. Our team aims to process all administrative requests within 2 business days.
