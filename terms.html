Section 01

Terms of Service

1. Architectural Consultancy Agreement

Signal5 is a managed cloud consultancy based in Ireland. By engaging our services, the Client acknowledges that Signal5 provides architectural configuration and managed optimization of client-owned Amazon Web Services (AWS) environments. This is a professional technical service, not a software product or utility.

2. Service Delivery & Verification

Delivery is defined as the successful provisioning of a Dedicated US Network Node. Final delivery is verified via a live Network Integrity Audit (onboarding session). Once the node is validated as active and consistent with the provisioned environment, the service is deemed fully rendered and completed.

3. Environment Integrity & Client-Side Compliance

The stability of the provisioned network relies on the Client maintaining a Controlled Hardware Environment. Signal5 is not liable for any "Origin-Network Exposure" (connectivity collisions), network identity inconsistencies, or third-party platform restrictions resulting from:

  • Hardware Configuration: Failure to maintain Hardware Level Isolation (active local cellular signals, physical SIM cards, or eSIMs).
  • Metadata Management: Failure to maintain Location Metadata Compliance (System-Level Location Services).
  • Software Tampering: Unauthorized modification of Client-side routing configurations or network protocols.
  • Fail-Safe Protocol: Improper use or deactivation of the enforced Network Fail-Safe Protocol.

4. Deployment Fee & Refund Policy (The Chargeback Shield)

Non-Refundable Labor: The €160 Deployment Fee is a service-based charge for professional technical labor. This fee is 100% non-refundable once the technical onboarding session has commenced, as specialized labor has been initiated.

Cancellation: Refunds are only eligible if requested via email at least 24 hours prior to the scheduled onboarding session.

Monthly Retainer: The €49 monthly management fee is billed in advance and is non-refundable once the billing cycle begins.

5. Infrastructure Ownership & Third-Party Costs

The Client maintains full ownership of the underlying AWS account. Signal5 is a technical manager only. All third-party infrastructure usage costs are the sole responsibility of the Client and are billed directly by the provider (AWS).

6. Limitation of Liability & Force Majeure

Signal5 is not liable for service interruptions caused by upstream providers (AWS), hardware failure, or changes in third-party platform algorithms. Our total liability is limited to the amount paid for the most recent service period.

7. No Partnership or Agency

Nothing in this agreement creates a partnership, joint venture, or employer-employee relationship. Signal5 acts as an independent contractor providing technical expertise.

Section 02

Privacy Policy

1. Data Sovereignty & Managed Infrastructure

Signal5 operates under a strict Managed Infrastructure Model. Unlike standard software-as-a-service (SaaS) providers, we do not own the network infrastructure. All provisioning occurs within the Client’s private Amazon Web Services (AWS) environment. This ensures the Client maintains total sovereignty and control over their own data and network traffic.

2. Zero-Retention Traffic Policy (Zero-Logs)

As a consultancy, Signal5 provides the architectural design but does not operate as a data carrier. We do not log, store, monitor, or inspect the content of the data traffic moving through your provisioned network node. Your traffic flows directly from your hardware to your isolated AWS environment; Signal5 has no technical visibility into your browsing history or data packets.

3. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data required to deliver our professional technical services:

  • Identification Data: Name and business email address for communication and billing.
  • Credential Data: Temporary AWS IAM credentials provided by the Client for the purpose of architectural deployment and health monitoring.
  • Billing Data: All financial transactions are processed securely via Stripe. Signal5 does not store or have access to your full credit card details or payment credentials.

4. Use of Third-Party Processors

To provide our professional services, we utilize industry-standard third-party providers:

  • Stripe: For secure, PCI-compliant payment processing.
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS): For hosting and infrastructure provisioning within the Client’s own account.

Signal5 does not sell, rent, or trade your data to any third-party marketing or data-mining firms.

5. Managed Maintenance & Oversight Access

To fulfill our obligations under the Managed Retainer, Signal5 maintains persistent IAM (Identity and Access Management) access to the Client’s provisioned AWS environment. This access is strictly utilized for operational oversight, including monitoring node uptime, performing health checks, and managing architectural fail-safes. We do not access Client data; our oversight is limited to the technical stability of the cloud infrastructure.

6. Your Rights (GDPR & Data Protection Act)

As an Irish-based consultancy, we comply with the Data Protection Acts 1988–2018 and the EU GDPR. You have the right to access, rectify, or request the erasure of your personal data held by us. For any data-related requests, please contact hello@signal5.io.

Section 03

Refund Policy

1. Setup & Technical Deployment Fee

The €160 initial payment is a service-based fee covering custom architectural design, 1-on-1 technical deployment labor, and your first 30 days of managed oversight.

  • Pre-Onboarding: You are eligible for a full refund if you cancel your engagement at least 24 hours before your scheduled FaceTime onboarding session.
  • Post-Onboarding: Once the FaceTime session has commenced and the US Network Presence has been live-validated on the call, the Setup Fee is 100% non-refundable. This is because the specialized professional labor has been fully rendered.

2. Managed Monthly Retainer

The €49 recurring fee covers ongoing node health monitoring, architectural updates, and fail-safe integrity checks. Retainer fees are billed in advance and are non-refundable once the billing cycle has started.

Cancellation: You can cancel your managed retainer at any time via your Stripe Customer Portal or by emailing support@signal5.io. Cancellation stops future billing, but no pro-rata refunds are issued for the current month.

3. Verification of Service

Upon completion of the live deployment, the Client will be asked to verify the active US Network Presence via email. This confirmation serves as the formal "Handover" of the provisioned environment. Once verified, the service is considered successfully delivered in full.
