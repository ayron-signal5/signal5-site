Network Compliance Standards

Six-point compliance walkthrough.

Every deployment is validated against our Network Compliance Standards on a live 1-on-1 FaceTime session — no assumptions, no self-service configuration.

01

Hardware Environment Sanitization

Prior to deployment, the client device undergoes a full environment reset. This eliminates residual configuration data, prior network fingerprints, and conflicting environment state that could compromise node integrity.

Settings → General → Erase All Content and Settings
02

Software-Defined Infrastructure (SDN)

Your US Network Presence is established via software-defined architecture provisioned within your AWS environment. No physical SIM card or external hardware is required or used.

Physical Hardware: Not Required · SDN: Active
03

Location Metadata Compliance

System-level location services are configured to prevent GPS-to-network-node mismatches that would compromise the integrity and consistency of your US Network Presence.

Privacy → Location Compliance: Verified
04

Private US DNS Resolution

All DNS queries resolve exclusively through private US-based resolvers provisioned within your cloud infrastructure. We conduct a full DNS chain audit to eliminate geographic resolution leaks.

DNS Resolver: US-Managed · Leak Test: Pass
05

Network Fail-Safe Protocol

We enforce an OS-level Network Fail-Safe Protocol that halts all data transfer in the event of node interruption, ensuring complete network environment isolation at all times.

Fail-Safe Level: OS Network Layer · Enforcement: Active
06

Full-Chain Environment Audit

We conduct a complete audit of your account regions, application configurations, and environment settings — verifying that your professional US Network Presence is consistent from the infrastructure layer to the application layer. Completed live on your onboarding FaceTime call.

Cloud Audit: Complete · Environment Consistency: Verified · Identity Layer: Confirmed
